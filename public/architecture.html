The Flow

1. You have conversations across WhatsApp, Gmail, Calendar, Slack, Telegram.
2. Your OpenClaw agent (Wazowski) observes these conversations — it has access to all channels.
3. The agent calls orbit_ingest to push participants, summaries, and cross-connections to Orbit.
4. Orbit stores Person nodes + INTERACTED edges + KNOWS edges in Neo4j, tenant-isolated by your userId.
5. You view the constellation on the dashboard, or ask the agent in Slack: "who's going cold?"
6. The agent calls orbit_going_cold and responds with actionable intelligence.
7. Every 4 hours, a cron job triggers the agent to sync recent activity into Orbit automatically.
8. IN PROGRESS: Server-side algorithm resolves identities across sources, scores relationships, and derives KNOWS edges with provenance.
